FAQ

Can I book an event?

Yes! We offer event bookings for birthdays, anniversaries, corporate gatherings. Contact us at events@harmonybar.com for more information and to discuss your specific requirements.

How can I make a reservation?

By calling at +123456789 or by using our online reservation form on our website.

What are your opening ours?

Our opening hours are from 5:00 PM to 4:00 AM, Tuesday through Sunday.

Do you have live music?

Yes, we have live music performances every night. Enjoy the soulful sounds of talented jazz musicians as you relax and unwind in our bar.

What is your dress code?

Our dress code is smart casual. We encourage our guests to dress stylishly and comfortably, while still maintaining an elegant and sophisticated atmosphere.
